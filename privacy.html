01 - Overview

What this policy is for

Plain English

beVivid is not presented as a large corporate platform. It is an independently run website and passion project, so this policy is meant to be honest, simple, and tied to what the site actually does.

In this Privacy Policy, "beVivid," "we," "us," and "our" refer to the beVivid website and related features offered through it.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when advertising or creator tools affect it, and what choices you have. By using beVivid, you agree to this policy.

02 - Information We Collect

What we may collect

We try to keep data collection tied to running the site and the features you choose to use.

  • Account details such as display name, handle, email address, password hash, account type, and legal-consent records you submit during signup.
  • Profile and community content such as bios, images, profile layout data, posts, comments, follows, likes, recognition, and notifications.
  • Creator-related data such as impression counts, like counts, comment counts, estimated earnings, and creator dashboard totals.
  • Application, support, or contact form information you send to the site.
  • Technical and usage information such as IP address, browser or device type, timestamps, page visits, and general interaction data.
03 - How Information Is Used

Why we use it

  • To create and maintain accounts, profiles, and login sessions.
  • To run community features such as posts, comments, follows, profile search, and notifications.
  • To measure engagement, impressions, recognition, and creator dashboard activity.
  • To respond to applications, contact requests, abuse reports, and support messages.
  • To improve the site, troubleshoot issues, and reduce spam, fraud, or abuse.
  • To show or measure ads if advertising is enabled on the site.

We do not say that the site will never use third-party tools. Hosting, forms, ads, analytics, storage, anti-abuse tools, or creator features may rely on third-party providers as the project grows.

04 - Ads, Cookies, and Analytics

How ads and cookies fit in

beVivid may show ads now or in the future. Those ads may be served by beVivid directly or by third-party advertising partners, including Google.

  • Cookies, local browser storage, and similar technologies may be used to keep you signed in, remember settings, measure traffic, serve ads, and understand performance.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve and measure ads based on visits to this site and other sites.
  • If you block cookies or scripts, some parts of the site may not work correctly.

You can learn more about Google's partner-site data practices here: How Google uses data when you use sites or apps that partner with Google.

You can also manage Google ad personalization here: Google Ads Settings.

05 - Public Sharing and Service Providers

When information is visible or shared

Some parts of beVivid are public by design.

  • Profile information, posts, comments, counts, and other content you choose to make public may be visible to other users or the public web.
  • We may use service providers for hosting, storage, forms, ads, analytics, security, audio or media delivery, and email or support tools.
  • We may share information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to abuse, or protect users or the site.

Examples of providers that may be used on or with beVivid include Firebase, Vercel, Google services, Brevo, and similar infrastructure or communication providers.

06 - Storage and Security

How long we keep data

We keep information for as long as it is reasonably useful for running the site, maintaining accounts, operating creator features, preventing abuse, or meeting legal obligations.

If you ask us to delete your account, we will try to remove or anonymize related data within a reasonable time, except where some records need to be kept for security, fraud prevention, accounting, moderation, or legal reasons.

We use reasonable steps to protect information, but no website or storage system can promise perfect security.

07 - Your Choices

Your options

  • You can edit profile details and content you control through your account tools.
  • You can ask for account deletion or contact us with privacy questions.
  • You can manage some cookies and tracking through your browser or device settings.

Because beVivid is independently run and still small, privacy and support requests may take some time, but we will try to respond in good faith.

08 - Age and Children's Privacy

Age limits

beVivid is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 was collected without appropriate permission, we will try to remove it.

If you are under 18, you should use the site with permission from a parent or guardian.

09 - Changes and Contact

Updates and contact

beVivid may change this Privacy Policy as the site grows, adds new features, or changes providers. When we update it, we will change the effective date at the top of this page.

Privacy Contact
