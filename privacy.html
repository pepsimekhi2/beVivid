01 — Introduction

Who We Are

Plain English

We built beVivid to celebrate people. That means we take your personal information seriously and handle it with care. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

beVivid ("beVivid," "we," "us," or "our") operates the beVivid platform, including our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Platform"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of every user — whether you're a student, a creator, or someone out here doing good in the world.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform. By accessing or using beVivid, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the Platform. Questions? Scroll to Section 12 or reach out anytime.

02 — Information We Collect

What We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways: information you give us directly, information generated by your use of the Platform, and (in limited cases) information from third-party sources.

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: name, email address, username, password, profile photo, and bio when you register.
  • Profile content: work samples, project descriptions, achievements, certifications, and any other content you choose to post.
  • Do Good activity: logs of volunteer work, community contributions, or good deeds you self-report on the Platform.
  • Student information: if you apply for a scholarship, we collect academic information and supporting materials you submit.
  • Payment information: if you subscribe to beVivid+, enroll in creator payouts, or otherwise make or receive payments through beVivid, payment details are processed by our third-party providers. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Communications: messages, support requests, or feedback you send to us.
  • Notification preferences: email address or phone number if you sign up for notifications.

Information collected automatically:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Device data: device identifiers, hardware model, and mobile network information.
  • Usage data: how you interact with the Platform — which features you use, trophies earned, awards viewed.
  • Advertising and monetization data: ad requests, ad impressions, ad interactions, approximate location inferred from IP, anti-fraud signals, creator analytics, and monetization eligibility or payout metrics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 5 for full details.
03 — How We Use Your Information

How We Use It

We use the information we collect to run, improve, and protect beVivid — and to give you the best possible experience on the Platform.

  • To create and manage your account and personalize your profile.
  • To process trophy awards, certifications, and recognition on the Platform.
  • To facilitate scholarship applications and connect students with opportunities.
  • To process payments for beVivid+ subscriptions.
  • To serve, limit, measure, and report advertising shown on the Platform, including preventing invalid traffic and ad fraud.
  • To calculate creator analytics, estimated earnings, payout eligibility, and any amounts we may pay under creator monetization programs.
  • To send you platform notifications, updates, and (where permitted) marketing communications.
  • To understand how the Platform is used and improve our features.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal data in the ordinary sense of handing over your identity for cash, but we may work with advertising, analytics, anti-fraud, and monetization partners who receive limited technical and usage data to help us operate ads and creator earnings on beVivid.

04 — How We Share Information

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the limited circumstances described below.

  • Publicly on your profile: Content you choose to publish on beVivid — your profile, work, awards, and Do Good logs — is visible to other users and potentially the public, depending on your privacy settings.
  • Service providers: We work with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processors, email delivery services) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Scholarship partners: If you apply for a scholarship, relevant application data may be shared with the scholarship sponsor, with your consent.
  • Advertising, analytics, and monetization partners: We may share limited technical, device, usage, and measurement data with ad networks, demand partners, ad measurement providers, anti-fraud vendors, or payout providers so they can serve ads, measure performance, prevent invalid traffic, and support creator monetization.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers: If beVivid is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before any such transfer takes effect.
  • With your consent: We may share data in other ways if you explicitly authorize us to do so.
05 — Cookies & Tracking

Cookies

beVivid uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how the Platform is used.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Platform to function (e.g., session cookies for authentication).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings (e.g., billing toggle preference, notification mode).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand usage patterns so we can improve the Platform.
  • Advertising and measurement cookies: Help us serve ads, cap frequency, measure impressions and engagement, attribute creator earnings, and detect invalid traffic or fraud.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality. Where required by law, we will also offer consent choices for non-essential analytics or advertising technologies.

06 — Data Retention

How Long We Keep It

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Platform. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, accounting, advertising compliance, creator payout, or other compliance reasons.

Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained indefinitely. Backup copies of deleted data may persist for up to 90 days before being permanently purged.

07 — Your Rights & Choices

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. Regardless of location, we offer the following to all users:

  • Access: You can view and download a copy of your personal data from your account settings.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your account information at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and request erasure of your data.
  • Portability: You can export your profile data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of communications: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email we send, or through your notification settings.
  • SMS opt-out: Reply STOP to any SMS message from beVivid to unsubscribe.
  • Ad controls: Where available, you may limit non-essential advertising or measurement technologies through browser settings, device controls, or consent tools we make available.

If you are a resident of California, the European Union, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights (e.g., CCPA, GDPR), additional rights may apply. Contact us at the address in Section 12 to exercise any of these rights.

08 — Children's Privacy

Children

beVivid is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that data promptly.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 should use the Platform with the knowledge and guidance of a parent or guardian. Certain features — including scholarship applications and subscription purchases — require users to be 18 or older, or to have parental consent.

09 — Security

Keeping You Safe

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your data — including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted storage of sensitive fields, and access controls limiting which team members can access personal data.

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights or freedoms, we will notify you and applicable regulators in accordance with applicable law.

10 — Third-Party Services

Third Parties

beVivid integrates with select third-party services to deliver our platform. These may include:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) for handling beVivid+ subscription billing.
  • Email and SMS delivery providers (e.g., Brevo) for notifications and communications.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and storage.
  • Analytics, advertising, and anti-fraud services for platform usage, ad delivery, measurement, and invalid traffic prevention.
  • Payout or financial services providers if creator monetization is enabled for your account.

These third parties are contractually required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and our instructions. We encourage you to review their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services that are linked from our Platform.

11 — Changes to This Policy

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If the changes are material, we will notify you by email or through a prominent notice on the Platform before the changes take effect.

Your continued use of beVivid after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
